About

Key Club is an organization that is branched from Kiwanis for High School students. In Key Club, students have the opportunity to serve, build character and develop leadership. They will be able to meet new people with common interests, and help the community to be part of the solution. Key Club is one of the most outstanding and oldest community service clubs around. Being a part of Key Club is a benefit for everyone, as they get to build bonds with each other and help serve their nation to make better communities for people to live in.

Pledge

I pledge on my honor, to uphold the objects of Key Club International.
To build my home, school, and community.
To serve my nation, and to combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.
